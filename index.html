The Internet

The Internet and related technologies provide tremendous new possibilities for doing business.

The Internet is creating a shared, real-time commercial space, the like of which has never been seen before.

But requires a fundamental rethink of traditional strategy and business models.

One of the most important developments to arise from the current swathe of technological advances is ecommerce

e-commerce

e-commerce refers to trading electronically: transactions involved with buying and selling products, services and information over a network.

Essentially with e-commerce, even a very small business can sell just as many products as a massive corporation.

With ecommerce one can:

  • Sell the exact right thing to the exact right person at the exact right time
  • Use multiple different channels
  • Incorporate things like social commerce and all kinds of other tools to sell much more

Companies like amazon and alibaba capture large revenues.
